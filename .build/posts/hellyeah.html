Hell Yeah or No

March 2, 2014 by Yan P Aung

Derek Sivers has been a tremendous source of inspiration to me, and I have always enjoyed reading his insightful blogs.

This post No more yes. It's either HELL YEAH! or no is by far one of my favorites. It's simple, the idea worth doing is the idea that you say 'Hell yeah' to. Otherwise, don't do it. Just say no.
